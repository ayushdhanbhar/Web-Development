Satkarma foundation is non profit organization dedicated to empowering underserved communities promoting education ,healthcare communities ,women empowerment .our founding in 2024 ,we have worked tirelessly to create lasting change through advocacy ,community driven initiatives and sustainable development. Our team is passionate about equality, opportunity and justice and we believe that by working together,we can create world where everyone has the chance to thrive. we are commited to making real difference in the lives of those who need it most.We envision a world where all children have access to quality education, the environment is protected for future generations, or everyone has the mental health support they need. Our goal is to make this vision a reality by collaborating with local partners, advocating for policy changes or implementing innovative programs. Join us on our mission to create a better future for all.
